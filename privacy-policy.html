AppNetOff Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026
Last Updated: March 24, 2026

AppNetOff is a privacy-first mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

AppNetOff does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. This includes:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone, location, etc.)
  • No usage data
  • No network data or logs
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs

2. Permissions Usage

The app uses the following permissions only for its core functionality:

  • INTERNET — Required for VPN service operation
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — To list installed apps for blocking
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — To keep VPN running
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — To detect WiFi/Mobile Data
  • BIND_VPN_SERVICE — To create VPN tunnel
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To restore schedules after reboot

3. Data Storage and Retention

All app settings and block rules are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

No data is sent to any external servers.

You can clear all data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

4. Third-Party Services

AppNetOff does not use any third-party services that collect user data. No analytics tools or external SDKs are used.

5. Security

Since no user data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of personal data exposure.

6. Your Rights

  • You can uninstall the app at any time
  • You can clear all app data from your device

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date.
